# Organization

Use Organization to manage the workspace your team shares in CVEalert. Monitoring, alerts, members, invitations, billing, limits, and integrations are scoped to the organization.

!!! tip
    Keep Admin access limited to people who need billing, member, or organization-level control.

## Common Tasks

### Review organization status

Use the overview to confirm the organization name, subscription, your role, member count, monitoring usage, alert usage, and integration status.

This is the best place to check whether the workspace is near plan or usage limits.

### Manage members

Members are users who already have access to the organization. Review their role, account status, and 2FA status as part of routine access reviews.

Remove members when they leave the team or no longer need access.

### Invite new members

Invite members by email and assign a role that matches their work. Invitations must be accepted before the member can access the organization.

Expired or incorrect invitations can be removed and recreated.

### Manage billing

Admins can open billing and invoices from the Organization page. Billing changes affect the organization, not only the individual account making the change.

## Notes

- Organization settings affect all members in the workspace.
- Member roles control what a person can view or change.
- Use [Roles](organization/roles/) when deciding which access level to assign.
